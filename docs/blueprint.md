# **App Name**: HostUp Pro

## Core Features:

- Centralized Staff & Room Management: Comprehensive system to manage all hotel rooms (status, type) and staff profiles (roles, departments, contact info).
- Service Request & Task Workflow: Enable guests and staff to create, assign, track, and close service requests across various categories (housekeeping, maintenance, towels, etc.).
- AI-Powered Request Triage Tool: An AI tool that automatically categorizes and suggests key details for service requests, especially 'other' requests, to streamline assignment and resolution.
- Interactive Operational Dashboard: A dynamic dashboard providing real-time insights into key operational metrics like room occupancy, pending tasks, staff activity, and performance trends.
- Staff Mobile Task Manager: A simplified mobile interface for housekeeping and maintenance staff to receive task assignments, update status, and mark completion with optional photo uploads.
- Guest Self-Service Kiosk: A tablet-optimized interface allowing guests to easily submit common requests like room cleaning, fresh towels, or maintenance issues directly from their room.
- Basic Reporting & Analytics: Generate essential reports including room occupancy, staff attendance, and service request completion rates to monitor operational efficiency.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional, deep blue (#2360DB) for a sense of trust, modernism, and operational excellence, reflecting the core theme.
- Background color: A subtle, almost white with a faint blue tint (#F6F8FA), ensuring a clean, expansive feel and allowing content to take center stage.
- Accent color: A vibrant indigo (#5238D1) to provide strong visual contrast for calls-to-action, alerts, and highlight crucial information within the light theme.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability across various screen sizes and its modern, neutral, and professional aesthetic suitable for SaaS dashboards and operational UIs.
- Leverage clean and universally recognizable icons adhering to Material Design 3 guidelines to ensure intuitive navigation and clear representation of actions and statuses.
- Emphasize a responsive layout with clear visual hierarchy, ample white space, and well-organized sections to enhance usability across web, mobile, and tablet interfaces, aligned with Modern SaaS Dashboard principles.
- Incorporate subtle and swift animations for transitions, status updates, and interactive elements to provide a smooth user experience and clear feedback without causing delays.
